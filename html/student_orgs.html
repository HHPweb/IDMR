Merchandising

The Apparel and Interior Merchandising Organization (AIMO) provides opportunities for students to learn more about careers, meet professionals in the field of merchandising, socialize with students who have similar interests, participate in service activities, organize annual fashion show, and participate in terrific field trips.

So far this year:

  • We have raised funds for Our Military Kids through ECU Military Appreciation T-shirt designed by our students
  • Our fifth annual fashion show entitled "Fashion is Queen" was a great success. The show presented current fashion trends from seven local boutiques and wearable art designs by eight students from ECU School of Art and Design.
  • We visited NYC to learn more about careers in merchandising and Atlanta to work at America's Mart.
  • AIMO donated its annual $500 towards Merchandising Scholarship.

Notice of AIMO meetings are posted near the departmental office on the second floor of the Rivers Building. Students interested in belonging to the organization are invited to attend meetings or contact the faculty adviser or president. All merchandising majors and minors are eligible to belong.
